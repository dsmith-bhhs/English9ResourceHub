English 9 Course Overview

Foundations of Literature & Writing

English 9 builds high school language arts mastery through close reading of fiction and informational texts, diverse writing modes (analytical essays, journal entries, creative works, spring research paper), grammar mini-lessons, 15 vocabulary units, and quarterly independent reading.
