TEMPLATES FOR WRITING SUMMARIES
To introduce a summary, use signal verbs (verbs that are more expressive than
“she says” or “the author believes”) in a template like these:
In her essay X, she advocates a radical revision of the juvenile
justice system.
They celebrate the fact that __________.
__________, he admits.
Here is a template that you can use to develop your summary:
X and Y, in their article __________, argue that __________. Their
research, which demonstrates that _____, challenges the idea that
__________. They use __________ to show __________. X and
Y’s argument speaks to __________ about the larger issue of
__________.
